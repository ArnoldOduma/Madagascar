FANORONA

HOW FANORONA IS PLAYED

  • The whites move first by sliding one space along one of the lines.
  • You can capture a line of your opponents pieces by approach by moving towards them into the adjacent space.
  • A turn consists of either a single, non-capturing move, or a sequence of capturing moves.
  • If any capturing moves are possible anywhere on the board, then one must be made.also if multiple captures are available, you can choose which one to execute.
  • Captures on the same turn are optional.

RULES

  • you must keep moving the same piece
  • you cannot return to any space twice
  • you can't move in the same direction twice in a row
